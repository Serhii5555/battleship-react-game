## GDPR Overview

### What is GDPR?

GDPR is a comprehensive data protection law in the European Union that governs how organizations collect, process, and store personal data. It replaces the 1995 Data Protection Directive and provides stronger privacy protections for individuals.

### Why GDPR Matters

GDPR establishes:

- **User Rights:** You control your personal data
- **Transparency:** Organizations must be clear about data use
- **Accountability:** Organizations are responsible for compliance
- **Security:** Data must be protected from misuse
- **Penalties:** Violations can result in significant fines

### Key Principles

GDPR is built on seven core principles:

1. **Lawfulness, Fairness, Transparency**
   - We process data lawfully and fairly
   - We're transparent about our practices
   - We explain how we use your data

2. **Purpose Limitation**
   - We collect data for specific purposes
   - We don't use it for incompatible purposes
   - We obtain new consent for new purposes

3. **Data Minimization**
   - We collect only necessary data
   - We don't collect excessive information
   - We minimize personal data collection

4. **Accuracy**
   - We keep your data accurate and up-to-date
   - We allow you to correct errors
   - We delete outdated information

5. **Storage Limitation**
   - We don't keep data longer than needed
   - We delete data when no longer necessary
   - We follow specific retention schedules

6. **Integrity and Confidentiality**
   - We protect data from unauthorized access
   - We prevent data breaches
   - We use appropriate security measures

7. **Accountability**
   - We document our data processes
   - We can prove GDPR compliance
   - We notify users of breaches

## Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject, you have **eight fundamental rights** under GDPR:

### 1. Right of Access (Article 15)

You have the right to obtain confirmation of whether we process your personal data and to request access to such data.

**What you can request:**

- Confirmation that we hold your data
- Copies of all personal data we have
- Information about how we use your data
- Details of who we share it with

**How to request:**

- Email: privacy@battleship-game.example.com
- Submit the access request form on our website
- Provide your full name and email

**Response time:** 30 days (may extend to 90 days if complex)

**Cost:** FREE - we process the first request at no charge

---

### 2. Right to Rectification (Article 16)

You have the right to correct inaccurate personal data and to complete incomplete data.

**What you can correct:**

- Your name or contact information
- Game preferences and settings
- Account information
- Any other inaccurate data

**How to correct:**

- Update directly in your account settings
- Email us: privacy@battleship-game.example.com
- Submit a rectification request

**Response time:** 30 days for verification and correction

---

### 3. Right to Erasure (Article 17)

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten") under certain circumstances.

**When you can request erasure:**

- The data is no longer necessary
- You withdraw your consent
- You object to processing
- The data is processed unlawfully
- Legal obligations require deletion
- You're a child (under 16) and consented online

**What gets deleted:**

- All personal data linked to your account
- Game history and statistics
- Preference settings
- Analytics data

**What doesn't get deleted:**

- Data required by law
- Data needed for legal claims
- Data archived for backup (temporarily)
- Anonymized data

**How to request:**

- Email: privacy@battleship-game.example.com
- Use the "Delete Account" option in settings
- Submit a formal deletion request

**Response time:** 30 days from request

**Note:** Some data may be retained for legal compliance or backup purposes for a limited time.

---

### 4. Right to Restrict Processing (Article 18)

You can request that we limit how we process your personal data while reviewing your request.

**When you can restrict:**

- You dispute the accuracy of your data
- The processing is unlawful
- We no longer need the data but you want retention
- You've objected to processing

**What happens when restricted:**

- We won't process your data for most purposes
- We'll keep it secure
- We'll notify you before lifting restrictions
- You consent to future processing

**How to request:**

- Email: privacy@battleship-game.example.com
- Specify the reason for restriction
- Ask for confirmation of restriction

---

### 5. Right to Data Portability (Article 20)

You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format and transfer it to another organization.

**What you can receive:**

- All personal data in JSON, CSV, or XML format
- Game preferences and settings
- Game history and statistics
- Account information

**Supported formats:**

- JSON (JavaScript Object Notation)
- CSV (Comma-Separated Values)
- XML (eXtensible Markup Language)
- Excel (.xlsx)

**How to request:**

- Email: privacy@battleship-game.example.com
- Use "Export Data" in account settings
- Specify desired format

**Response time:** 30 days from request

**Cost:** FREE

---

### 6. Right to Object (Article 21)

You have the right to object to processing of your personal data for specific purposes.

**You can object to:**

- Marketing communications
- Analytics and tracking
- Profiling
- Any processing based on legitimate interest

**You cannot object to:**

- Processing necessary for contract fulfillment
- Processing required by law

**How to object:**

- Email: privacy@battleship-game.example.com
- Unsubscribe link in marketing emails
- Use preferences in account settings

**Response time:** 30 days from receipt

---

### 7. Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making (Article 22)

You have the right not to be subject to decisions based solely on automated processing.

**Automated processing includes:**

- Algorithmic decision-making
- Profiling
- AI-based decisions

**Your rights:**

- Right to human intervention
- Right to express your point of view
- Right to obtain explanation

**How to request:**

- Email: privacy@battleship-game.example.com
- Ask for human review of decisions
- Request explanation of automated decisions
